About Us

ELIXER MULTI-PURPOSE COOPERATIVE is primarily engaged in providing Auxiliary Manpower services, with savings and lending operations for all members established and coowned by its members numbering to more than 4,000 nationwide who work as service crews, bartenders, sales and marketing personnel, cooks, cashiers, busboys, janitors, crew members, liaison and delivery personnel, office staff, clerks, supervisors and similar types of qualified personnel who perform the cooperative’s service engagements with its clients. We have served the manpower needs of some of the country’s biggest companies since March 2006 and still considered to be in business with them primarily because we offer them hassle free administration and cost effective benefits.
